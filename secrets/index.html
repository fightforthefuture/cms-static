Think you have nothing to hide?

Thanks to outdated privacy laws, the government claims they can read anything in your email, facebook messages, or chat history without a warrant.

So what would they see if they searched your Gmail?

You might not think you have anything in your email that could get you into trouble. But are you sure? Ever smoked pot? Or talked politics online? Looked at embarrassing porn? Or shared secrets with a friend or lover? You might be surprised what's in your email that you'd rather keep private. Use the links below search your own email (you have to be logged into Gmail in a separate window for this to work.) After you try them, can you do us a huge favor and send us some feedback on how the searches worked for you so we can make them better? Just email us: team@fightforthefuture.org
