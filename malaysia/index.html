Internet access is a human right. And it's essential for fair elections.

Free speech and open communications are essential to fair elections and democratic culture. That's why we can't let any government or powerful institution censor the open Internet.

Malaysian groups are using the open Internet to crowdsource election fraud cases to ensure people get to vote for their candidate and investigating and rapidly responding to site blocking and filtering.

Malaysian citizens deserve fair elections, but the government is hell bent on shutting down the internet to make sure that doesn't happen. No one reading this should be okay with that.
