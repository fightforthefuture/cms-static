Comcast-Ghostwriter

Emails obtained through a public records request reveal that Comcast is ghost-writing endorsement letters from politicians to the FCC in support of the Comcast-Time Warner Cable merger. These personal letters of support from several politicians claim to represent their respective communities, when they are actually PR letters for Comcast, written by Comcast.

These government officials betrayed our deepest trust when they sent Comcast's letters supporting the merger, which will affect millions by allowing the cable giants, Comcast and TWC, to further monopolize Internet access. Their words carry weight with agencies like the FCC because they are supposed to be representing our opinions and needs. Allowing Comcast to write letters for them, supporting a merger that will affect us all is a betrayal to the public. Call for the resignation of the politicians who sent Comcast's letters.
