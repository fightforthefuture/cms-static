Time to kick the cops out of your email!

Everyone knows about the NSA, but an outdated law called ECPA makes it way too easy for literally hundreds of government agencies to read your email -- from the IRS to local cops. There's a bill moving through Congress right now that would fix this, but it needs a little help from us.

Tell your lawmakers to cosponsor the Email Privacy Act!

The government should have to get a warrant to read your email. Period. Over 180 members of Congress agree and are cosponsoring bipartisan common sense legislation to fix ECPA. Email your reps today to make sure they do too.
