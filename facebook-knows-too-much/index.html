Facebook has gone too far!

WHAT IS FACEBOOK THINKING? Recently, the social networking site began collecting data on every action you take while on the site - even your cursor position. This means that Facebook doesn't just know about the links you clicked... it knows the links you thought about clicking! We need to tell them to KNOCK IT OFF!

Tell Facebook: Stop spying on my cursor!

Everyone knows that Facebook collects data - what you like, what you say, and who you're friends with. But now they want even more - like where your mouse is positioned on the screen. They will know which links you were thinking about clicking on, and who you were thinking about messaging. We can't let this happen, because if Facebook knows...

...then the NSA knows. One of the best way to prevent the NSA from knowing what you do online is to stop the companies from storing your data to begin with. Facebook's new collection policy is just in the testing phase - if we let them know how outraged we are, we can stop it BEFORE it becomes company policy!
