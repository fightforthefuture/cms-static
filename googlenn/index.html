Are Google lobbyists killing net neutrality?

Google helped pioneer open Internet principles, but their new party-line lobbyists are leading them astray and keeping them silent on real net neutrality. They need to get out of the way.

Tell Google execs: "Don't let your top lobbyists sell out the Open Internet, stand up for Title II net neutrality."

The only legal way to make sure there are no fast or slow lanes on the Internet, as Google itself has advocated for, is to urge the FCC to reclassify ISP's as telecommunications services. As the FCC moves to make a decision in December, we can make sure they know where Google stands --- with net neutrality and Title II.
