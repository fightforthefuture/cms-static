The guy that helped write SOPA might just kill our best chance at real NSA reform.

The USA FREEDOM Act is a bill that would limit some of NSA's bulk spying operation. SOPA author Bob Goodlatte and House Majority Leader Eric Cantor hold the most influence over this bill and could move it to a vote - in which case it would most likely pass. Instead, they've been dragging their feet, and now there's real danger that two fake NSA reform bills could take control of the debate in the House and give the government MORE power to invade our privacy. We need everyone in Virginia to call their reps right now.
