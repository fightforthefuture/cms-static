What is the Coalition?

We just had a big win with net neutrality, true. But, in order to keep winning, we need to stay on the offensive. And part of being on the offense means leading the conversation and owning the message.
That is where you come in.
Artists, musicians, filmmakers, writers - your art is what makes people sit up and pay attention. Your work engages and connects people in ways that can transform our society.
In order to protect creative freedom on the internet and in all its forms, we need you to join in this fight.
This Coalition is the first step towards a beautiful, messy, future we are building together.

Who is Fight for the Future?

We are a small but mighty team of activists and artists who have been educating and organizing Internet advocates to defend a free and open Internet since 2011.
Our vision is of a world where people can use the new transformative powers of the Internet to shape the world around them and come together to ensure basic rights and freedom in their lives, without censorship and barriers.
Ever since we organized the first protest against SOPA in 2011 -- we've been on a winning streak. We're best known for our massive, viral organizing -- and unconventional strategies -- redefining what's possible using the Internet.
Recent BIG wins include:

    2015: FCC passed Title II rules to ensure Net Neutrality thanks to massive battleforthenet.com campaign

    2014: Thousands of sites and hundreds of thousands of people participated in our #InternetSlowDown campaign
    2013: Organized the StopWatching.Us campaign against NSA surveillance with other grassroots organizations after the Edward Snowden leaks
    2012: Killed SOPA (Stop Online Piracy Act) in Congress by organizing the Internet blackout campaign.

So join us.
As part of the Coalition, you will be kept updated with the latest efforts Fight for the Future is working on that affect you and your creative peers.
FFTF will also let you know about ways to contribute your art to the cause. Because we know that art can change everything.
